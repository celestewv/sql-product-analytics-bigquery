# Executive Case Study

## Objective

Evaluate performance, funnel efficiency, marketing ROI, and retention opportunities using SQL analysis.

---

## Key Findings

### Funnel

- Home → Basket: 10.9%
- Basket → Payment: 24.9%
- Payment → Purchase: 65.2%

Largest friction occurs early in the journey.

---

### Channel Performance

Paid channels (cpm, cpc) show ~2x conversion efficiency compared to organic.

Referral traffic drives volume but low purchase rate.

---

### Retention

Return Rate: 13.1%

Low repeat engagement suggests opportunity for lifecycle marketing improvements.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Improve homepage-to-cart engagement
2. Introduce personalized onboarding nudges
3. Reallocate budget toward high-performing paid channels
4. Implement re-engagement campaigns for first-time buyers
